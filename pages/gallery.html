Key Facilities Highlight

Electrical Machinery Lab

Spacious, fully ventilated lab housing three-phase alternators, slip-ring induction motors, DC shunt motors, transformers, and earthing setups.

Civil Drafting Studio

Fitted with ergonomic drawing tables, surveying instruments (Total Stations, Auto-levels, prismatic compasses), and high-spec CAD computers.

Electronic Diagnostics Lab

Modern ESD-safe workbench stations containing digital CROs, function generators, microcontrollers, and complete PCB etching units.

Reference Library

Houses over 2,000 reference manuals, textbooks, industrial safety guides, trade directories, and subscription scientific journals.
