Preparation

Our Placement Process

We follow a structured methodology to transform students into industry-ready technicians.

1. Vocational Training

Providing rich, hands-on practice in state-of-the-art workshops under the supervision of expert faculty.

2. Aptitude & Skills Assessment

Regular mock tests, assignments, and curriculum evaluations to monitor and elevate skill levels.

3. Mock Interviews

Interview preparation, resume drafting, and personal grooming workshops conducted by experts.

4. Final Placement

On-campus and off-campus recruitment drives connecting students to leading employers.
