"At M.E.S. ITI Mundur, we are committed to providing outstanding vocational training that bridges the gap between rural education and modern industrial demand. Our students receive rigorous hands-on practice, safety training, and personal development to excel as skilled tech professionals globally."

Dr. P.K. Abdul Ghafoor Memorial M.E.S. Private Industrial Training Institute was established under the prestigious banner of the Muslim Educational Society (MES) Trust. Our prime objective is to offer qualitatively high-standard vocational training that helps local youth acquire job-ready technical skills.

Our Heritage

The Muslim Educational Society

The Muslim Educational Society (MES) was founded in Calicut in 1964 under the dynamic leadership of the late Dr. P.K. Abdul Ghafoor. What began as a modest educational initiative has grown into one of the largest educational organizations in the country, managing more than 150 institutions across India.

MES operates medical colleges, engineering colleges, arts and science colleges, technical training centers, and schools. We cater to the educational aspirations of all segments of society, with a particular focus on empowering backward and rural communities through professional and technical skills development.

Our ITI campus in Mundur, Palakkad carries forward this rich heritage, keeping vocational training affordable, accessible, and high-quality.
