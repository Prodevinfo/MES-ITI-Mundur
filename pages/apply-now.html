Admissions Guidelines

Admissions to various NCVT-accredited trades at Dr. P.K. Abdul Ghafoor Memorial M.E.S. Private ITI Mundur are conducted in strict compliance with the guidelines laid down by the Directorate General of Training (DGT), Government of India, and the State Directorate of Industrial Training Kerala.

Step-by-Step Admission Process

1

Online Enquiry

Submit the online enquiry form below with your educational details and trade preferences.

2

Counseling & Document Review

Our counseling team will contact you to review your qualifications and arrange a campus visit.

3

Interview & Verification

Attend a short aptitude interview at the ITI along with original copies of certificates.

4

Seat Allocation

Confirm admission by paying the tuition fees and submitting the required documents.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Electrician10th (SSLC) Pass with Math/Science
  • Draughtsman Civil10th (SSLC) Pass with Math/Science
  • Electronic Mechanic10th (SSLC) Pass with Math/Science
  • Wireman8th Class Pass Minimum
  • Plumber8th Class Pass Minimum
  • Minimum Age14 Years Completed

Required Documents

  • SSLC Certificate / 8th Class Transfer Certificate
  • Conduct and Character Certificate
  • Recent Passport Size Photos (5 Nos)
  • Aadhaar Card copy
  • Caste/Community Certificate (if claiming concession)
